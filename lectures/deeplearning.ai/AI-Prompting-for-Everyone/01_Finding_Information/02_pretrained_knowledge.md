# Pretrained Knowledge

## What is Pretraining?
- 🔑 **Pretrained knowledge** — what AI learns from reading data
- AI learns like children: by **reading a lot**
- One of the **first steps** of AI training
- ⚠️ "Pretraining" is just a technical name — don't overthink it
- 💡 Understanding sources helps **predict AI behavior**

## Training Data Sources
- **Internet forums & social media**
  - Reddit, Quora
  - *e.g. "must-watch films?"*
- **Books**
  - *e.g. Lego micro cities*
- **Encyclopedias**
  - Wikipedia (*e.g. fairy bread*)
- **News articles**
- **Research articles**
- 📊 **Trillions to tens of trillions of words**

## What AI Can Do
- **Answer everyday questions**
  - *"I dropped my phone in soup"*
  - *"Why do cats stare at walls?"*
    - AI-added: cats detect subtle sounds & movements humans miss
- **Possess niche knowledge**
  - *Voyager 1 vinyl record*
    - NASA spacecraft, launched **1970s**
    - ~25 billion miles from Earth
    - Greetings in **55 languages**
- **Handle messy input**
  - ✅ Understands **misspellings** & typos
  - ✅ Robust to bad grammar
  - 💡 Don't waste time fixing every typo
  - ⚠️ Not an excuse to write badly on purpose

## Frequency Shapes Reliability
- 💡 **Rule of thumb:** more frequent topic → more reliable answer
- **High-frequency topics**
  - Cooking
  - Celebrities
  - Movies
- **Low-frequency topics**
  - 📌 *Quasars* — bright objects powered by supermassive black holes
  - Specialized / technical subjects
- **Languages**
  - Mostly **English**
  - 📊 Cantonese: 80M+ speakers, **<0.1%** of internet content
  - Other languages underrepresented
- **Things AI knows nothing about**
  - ❌ Your company's proprietary data
  - ❌ Private / non-public information

## Limitations & Caveats
- ⚠️ Internet contains **misconceptions**
- ⚠️ Internet contains **outdated information**
- ⚠️ Pretrained knowledge **not enough** for real-time info
  - 📌 Need **web search** for current data *(next video)*
- 💡 Skill: **prompt carefully** to reduce misconceptions

## Key Takeaways
- 🔑 **Pretrained knowledge** = patterns AI learned from massive internet text
- 📌 Reliability tracks **frequency in training data** — common topics > niche topics
- ✅ AI is robust to typos, misspellings, and casual grammar
- ⚠️ Beware **misconceptions, outdated info, and missing private data**
- 💡 Knowing the sources helps you **predict and prompt** the AI better
- 📌 For real-time info, supplement with **web search**
