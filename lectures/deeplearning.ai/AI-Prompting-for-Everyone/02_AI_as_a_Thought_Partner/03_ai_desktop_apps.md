# AI Desktop Apps

## What They Are
- Beyond chat interfaces
- can **Agentically gathering context** 
  - from web searching
  - from your computer
  - ask you for contex(e.g. addtional info, decisions)
- Examples
  - *Claude Cowork*
  - *Microsoft Copilot Cowork*
  - *Google Antigravity*
- 💡 Find & read files to self-supply needed context
- Operate **with your permission**

## How They Work
- Powered by an **AI model**
  - Brings **pretrained knowledge**
- Built-in **tools**
  - `web_search` — fetch external info
  - File operations on your computer
    - Search files
    - Read files
    - Write files
    - Move & rename files
- 🔑 **Agentic** — chooses which tools to use per task

## Common Use Case: Folder Organization
- **Scenario** — messy research folder
  - PDFs, images, reports
- **Workflow**
  - Ask AI to organize the folder
  - AI explores file names & contents
  - AI proposes initial reorganization
  - You review & give feedback
  - AI returns a **refined proposal**
  - On approval, AI executes
    - Renames files
    - Moves files
    - Creates subdirectories

## Best Practice Workflow
- 📌 **Plan → Review → Execute**
- Step 1 — State the task
- Step 2 — Let AI **propose a plan** (no action yet)
- Step 3 — Review & critique
- Step 4 — Iterate on the plan
- Step 5 — Approve, then let AI execute
- Can iterate futher to review and critique the result
- ✅ Keeps human in control of irreversible actions

## Context: Chat vs Desktop App
- **AI Chat**
  - You must decide in advance what files to upload
  - ⚠️ Limited to context you pre-select
- **AI Desktop App**
  - Launches in a chosen folder
  - **Auto-explores** files as needed
  - Loads only relevant files → manages context efficiently
- 💡 Example — "write a filming schedule this week"
  - AI scans `Documents/filming`
  - Notices crew member *Mia's* birthday that week
  - Folds celebration into the schedule

## Using Desktop Apps Safely
- ⚠️ Apps can **read, edit, and delete** your files
- Mishaps are rare but **have happened**
- **Scope the access**
  - ❌ Don't launch in your home folder
  - ✅ Launch in the smallest relevant subfolder
- **Review permission requests carefully**
  - Know exactly what gets read & written
- ⚠️ Deleted files often **bypass the recycle bin**
  - May be unrecoverable
- ⚠️ Edited files usually have **no edit history**
  - Can't roll back unwanted changes
- 📌 Be extra cautious until familiar with the tool

## Why It Matters
- Enables AI to **discover relevant context** on its own
- Enables AI to **take real actions** on your files
- 💡 Rich context unlocks better **reasoning tasks**
  - Tasks where AI thinks longer for better answers
- Bridges to next topic — **Reasoning with AI**

## Key Takeaways
- 💡 Desktop apps shift AI from passive chat to **active collaborator** on your filesystem
- 🔑 **Agentic file exploration** removes the burden of pre-selecting context
- 📌 Should follow **Plan → Review → Execute** before letting AI act
- ⚠️ Scope folder access narrowly — deletions and edits may be **irreversible**
- ✅ More relevant context → better reasoning and better results
