# Working with Multimedia Data

## Beyond Text: Multi-Modal AI
- 🔑 **Multi-modal** — outputs/inputs with multiple modalities
- Goes beyond just text generation
- ⚠️ Slower and more costly than text
- 💡 Unlocks much more of what AI can do

## Output Modalities
### 🖼️ Images
- Creative & brainstorming tool
- *Example:* Nova's 7th birthday cake
  - AI-generated cat cake design
  - Used **NanoBanana** (Google)
  - Baker rendered it into real 3D cake
- 💡 Great for exploring design options

### 🎬 Video
- Previously needed expensive special effects
- *Example:* "Man shrinking" team demo
- Modern AI syncs video with generated audio

### 🎙️ Voice / Audio
- Voice **cloning** from a sample
- *Example:* Andrew's voice clone reading The Batch
- 📌 Even close family may not detect clones
- Music generation also possible

### 💻 Code
- *Example:* Yellow cat-themed typing game for Nova
- Makes basic programming accessible to everyone
- ✅ Lowers barrier to building real apps

## Input + Output Combinations
- **Text + Image → Text**
  - Upload Halloween costume photo
  - AI brainstorms alien-inspired ideas
- **Text + Audio → Text + Video**
  - Upload creepy sounds
  - AI designs haunted house with synced audio
- 💡 Inputs are flexible; outputs vary in cost

## Cost & Speed of Generation
### Input Cost
- Most input types are roughly comparable
- Differences are minor

### Output Cost (low → high)
- 📊 **Text** — fastest, cheapest
  - 🔑 **LLMs** = Large Language Models
  - AI started here, highly optimized
- 📊 **Speech** — more expensive than text
- 📊 **Images** — more expensive than speech
- 📊 **Video** — *much* more expensive than all others
- ⚠️ Time scales with cost — right = slower & pricier

## Progress Over Time
### Image Generation
- **2022:** Google *Imagen* — visible artifacts
  - Wrong lines on walls
  - Dishes morphed mid-wash
- **Today:** Near-photorealistic, audio-synced

### Voice Generation
- **Past:** Robotic, flat, inexpressive
- **Today:** Expressive, natural-sounding

## Applying Prompting Techniques
- ✅ **Give enough context** — works the same
- ✅ **Use the best model available** — works the same
- ⚠️ **Generate multiple options** — harder
  - Each option takes seconds to minutes
- ⚠️ **Iterate through designs** — harder
  - Costly and time-consuming
- 💡 With patience, all techniques still apply

## Responsible Use
### Beneficial Uses
- ✅ Podcast fixes (re-synth flubbed words)
- ✅ Lifelike voices in video games
- ✅ Empowers small/indie developers

### Harmful Uses
- ❌ Voice-clone scams
- ❌ Impersonating relatives in fake emergencies
- ⚠️ Raises concerns for voice actors' livelihoods

### Guiding Principle
- 💡 Beneficial uses vastly outnumber harmful ones
- 📌 Each of us should use AI **responsibly**

## Key Takeaways
- 💡 AI is now **multi-modal** — text, images, audio, video, code
- 📌 **Text is cheapest; video is most expensive** to generate
- 🔑 Most prompting techniques transfer to multi-modal work
- ⚠️ Iteration is harder when each output is slow/costly
- ✅ Use multi-modal AI for creative, beneficial, responsible applications
- 📌 Next up: **using images as input** so AI can *see* what you mean
